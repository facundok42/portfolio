# Facundo Kaper

Data has always been about stories for me: what's actually happening, why it's happening, and what to do about it. After 10+ years working across media, telecoms, enterprise, and fintech, I've learned that the most valuable skill isn't mastering any single tool, but knowing how to listen to what the data is trying to say and communicating that clearly to the people who need to hear it.
I've built dashboards that replaced hours of manual reporting, defined event taxonomies from scratch, automated workflows that freed up entire teams, and sat across the table from stakeholders to turn vague questions into clear, actionable analysis. My toolkit includes SQL, Python, Power BI, Tableau and BigQuery among others, and i'm always trying to improve on my options. 
My favorite part of my work is when raw data becomes something tangible, when data becomes information. 
This portfolio is a collection of projects that started with a messy question and became a story.
